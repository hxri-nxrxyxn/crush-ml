# ex116: The Transformer Architecture

### Concept
"Attention is All You Need." Transformers replaced RNNs entirely. They use **Self-Attention** to process the whole sequence at once (parallelism).

### Why they won
- **Speed**: Much faster than RNNs because they don't process step-by-step.
- **Long-range context**: They can easily connect words at the beginning and end of a long book.
- **Foundation**: This is the architecture behind BERT, GPT, and modern LLMs.
