# ex065: Kernel PCA (kPCA)

### Concept
Just like the "Kernel Trick" in SVMs, kPCA allows you to perform complex non-linear projections for dimensionality reduction.

### Use Case
It is often used to "unroll" twisted manifolds (like the Swiss Roll dataset). 

### Popular Kernels
- **RBF (Gaussian)**
- **Polynomial**
- **Sigmoid**
